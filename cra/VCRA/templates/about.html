About CivicEye

CivicEye is a public platform where citizens can report and track civic issues such as potholes, garbage accumulation, streetlight outages, and more.

Our goal is to bridge the communication gap between citizens and local authorities by enabling transparent issue reporting and resolution.

We believe in the power of community. Together, we can make our cities cleaner, safer, and better to live in.
